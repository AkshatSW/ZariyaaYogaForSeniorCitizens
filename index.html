So What Are You Waiting For?

What will you experience in this journey?

These Mindfulness sessions will be conducted from Monday to Saturday on three time slots. You can attend any that you wish depending on your availability.


The sessions that you will experience are :-

Chair Yoga

Chair yoga is a gentle form of yoga that can be done while sitting.This type of yoga can improve your flexibility, concentration and strength, while boosting your mood, and reducing stress and joint strain.

Laughter Therapy

We will use laughter as a healing agent to bust out the stress and negative emotions. The session will consist of various types of laughter therapies, along with some Breathwork.

Sound Healing

Sound healing will help you unlock a new multidimensional version of yourself with profound results and immerse yourself in sound and sonic vibrations which will help you relax and be in the present moment.

Breath Work

In this session you will experience the power of various advanced breathing techniques, learn ways to process unpleasant emotions and tackle them effectively and finally, crack the secret towards developing higher self-awareness

Meditation Series

Our mediation series has a list of various meditative practices for you to increase your natural vitality.This series consists of meditation techniques that will release your pent up emotions, Boost immunity, decrease chronic pains and more.
