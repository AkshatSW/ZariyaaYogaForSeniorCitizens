What will the senior citizens experience on this journey?

The sessions that they will experience are carefully curated keeping their age and health conditions in mind.

Chair Yoga

Chair yoga is a gentle form of yoga that can be done while sitting. This yoga improves their flexibility, concentration and strength, while boosting their mood, reducing stress and joint strain.

Laughter Yoga

Laughter yoga is a cardiovascular exercise and acts as a healing agent to bust out the stress and negative emotions. The session will consist of various types of laughter therapies, and breathwork.

Sound Healing

Sound healing will help them in unlocking a new multidimensional version of themselves with profound results, as well as immersing themselves in sound and sonic vibrations allowing them to relax & be in the present moment.

BreathWork

Here they will experience the power of various advanced breathing techniques, to stay centred and calm, process unpleasant emotions effectively and crack the secret towards developing higher self-awareness

Meditation Series

We will use list of unique meditation techniques to release their pent up emotions, Boost immunity, decrease chronic pains and detox and energise the body and mind to connect with the higher consciousness.
