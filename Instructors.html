Subramanian Vishwanathan

Do you know what the most important quality a guru must have? It's humour. And Subramanian sir is undoubtedly the most amusing and inspiring guru, who is extremely proficient in his teachings.

He teaches yoga in a holistic manner to assist his students in maintaining perfect health on all levels: physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual. For over 30 years, he has touched the hearts of people all over the world. Throughout his journey, he has been able to heal a large number of people suffering from chronic sickness and agony using his well-known laughter therapy and other yogic practises.

Despite his age, he is still a child at heart and as fit as a teenager. We are confident that you will adore him. He is Zariyaa's most popular expert, and his sessions are always packed to capacity since everyone wants to be a part of his magnetism.

He is extremely skilled in his subject, having passed the quality council of India's exam for yoga experts. Some of his qualifications are :

  • Certified sound therapist
