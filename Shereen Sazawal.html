Shereen Sazawal

From a young age, Shereen was fascinated with human behaviour, She has pursued her education in Psychology, got an MBA and worked as a talent manager with leading MNCs.

Then she found her calling in designing and delivering training to help people in their journey of personal and professional growth.

She wanted to make a difference and create a long-lasting impact, but the traditional training methodology of presentations was just not enough. She realised that to bring about real change one needs to learn cognitively, physically, emotionally and psychologically.
